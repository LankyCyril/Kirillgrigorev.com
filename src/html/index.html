Hi, I'm a PhD student at Weill Cornell Medicine in NYC working on NASA genomics data, multispecies comparative transcriptomics, and hybrid genome assembly.
I have a master's degree from the University of Puerto Rico and a bachelor's from the Saint Petersburg Chemical and Pharmaceutical Academy (Russia).
I maintain a few projects on GitHub: snakecharmer, pyvenn, bwakite, and pdfpayload.
Very occasionally, I write technology oriented blog posts, mainly about data science and tinkering with Raspberry Pi for fun and for sound processing.
Even more occasionally, I play guitar and piano, and I have yet to upload anything to my SoundCloud. A couple of videos that I shot in Puerto Rico are about to be released on my YouTube page.
